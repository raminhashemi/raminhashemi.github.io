Introduction

My name is Ramin Hashemi, I was born on 22 of September 1994, Ardebil, Iran. I am currently a PhD student with a major in wireless communications at Oulu University, Finland.

During my BSc studies I was fascinated by electrical components and building amazing circuits inlcluding communication components such as FM/AM. My BSc project is about investigating nonlinear effects in fiber optics which is vastly employed in continental communications. As a matter of fact, fiber optical links are secure, high speed which does not have detrimental effects on the nature and humanity.

I have extensive experiences in optimizing the performance of wireless networks. During my MSc studies, I have worked as an expert in wireless communication networking problems and challenges. My research interests include resource allocation in wireless networks, non-convex and nonlinear optimization, massive MIMO and fiber-wireless networks.
